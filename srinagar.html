📍 Location

Srinagar is the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, located in the beautiful Kashmir Valley surrounded by the majestic Himalayas. Situated along the Jhelum River at approximately 1,600 meters altitude, it is easily accessible by air, rail, and road.

📚 History & Importance

Srinagar has immense historical importance dating back to ancient times. The city witnessed the glory of Mughal emperors who were captivated by its beauty and developed stunning gardens. It has been a center of learning, art, and culture throughout history, with Kashmiri heritage of literature, craftsmanship, and spirituality still flourishing today.

✨ Features

Srinagar is famous for its serene Dal Lake, traditional houseboats offering unique accommodation, and magnificent Mughal gardens.

  • Dal Lake - Scenic lake with shikara boat rides and floating gardens
  • Nishat Bagh - Beautiful Mughal garden cascading down mountainside
  • Shalimar Bagh - Historic garden with terraced fountains and pavilions
  • Hazratbal Mosque - Sacred white marble mosque with spiritual significance

🎯 Why Visit Srinagar?

Known as "Paradise on Earth," Srinagar offers breathtaking landscapes and unique experiences found nowhere else. Experience houseboat stays with stunning water views, explore lush gardens, enjoy shikara rides, and shop for authentic Kashmiri handicrafts.

  • Houseboat Experience - Stay on traditional wooden boats in Dal Lake
  • Shikara Rides - Traditional boat rides through scenic waterways at sunset
  • Mughal Gardens - Walk through centuries-old landscaped gardens
  • Local Shopping - Authentic Kashmiri carpets, shawls, and handicrafts

🗺️ Must-Visit Nearby Places
