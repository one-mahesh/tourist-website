📍 Location

Agra is located in Uttar Pradesh on the banks of the Yamuna River, about 200 km from Delhi. It is easily accessible by train, air, and road from major Indian cities.

📚 History & Importance

Agra was the capital of the Mughal Empire during the reign of emperors like Akbar, Jahangir, and Shah Jahan. It is famous for its magnificent Mughal architecture and historical monuments representing the pinnacle of Mughal artistic achievements.

✨ Features

Agra is home to the world-famous Taj Mahal, a symbol of love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

  • Taj Mahal - Iconic white marble monument of eternal love and beauty
  • Agra Fort - Red sandstone fortress with rich Mughal history
  • Fatehpur Sikri - Abandoned Mughal city with stunning architecture
  • Yamuna River - Scenic walks and sunset views from river ghats

🎯 Why Visit Agra?

Agra is perfect for history lovers and tourists interested in architecture. The Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and attracts visitors globally for its breathtaking beauty.

  • Taj Mahal - Marvel at one of the Seven Wonders of the World
  • Mughal Heritage - Experience grand Mughal architecture and history
  • Fort Exploration - Discover the strategic Agra Fort's intricate design
  • Romantic Atmosphere - Symbol of eternal love attracts couples worldwide
