🏔 Bhrigu Lake

Location

Bhrigu Lake is located at an altitude of around 4,300 meters near Manali in Himachal Pradesh. It can be reached by trekking from Gulaba.

History & Importance

The lake is named after Maharishi Bhrigu, who is believed to have meditated here. It is considered sacred and holds religious significance.

Features

The lake is surrounded by snow-covered mountains and lush green meadows. It remains frozen during winters and offers breathtaking views.

Why Visit

Bhrigu Lake is ideal for trekking and adventure lovers. The scenic beauty and peaceful environment make it a must-visit destination.

Nearby Places

Gulaba, Rohtang Pass, Solang Valley, Nehru Kund
