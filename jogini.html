🌄 Jogini Falls

Location

Jogini Falls is located near Vashisht Village, about 3 kilometers from Manali in Himachal Pradesh. It lies in the scenic Kullu Valley and can be reached by a short trek starting from the Vashisht Temple. The route passes through dense pine forests, apple orchards, and small streams, offering a beautiful natural experience.

History & Importance

Jogini Falls holds religious significance as it is dedicated to the local village deity "Jogini." The place is considered sacred, and villagers often perform rituals near the waterfall. It reflects the cultural beliefs and traditions of Himachal Pradesh.

Features

The waterfall drops from a height of around 150–160 feet and creates a stunning view. The surroundings include lush greenery, rocky terrain, and peaceful natural beauty. Small water pools are formed at the base, and the trekking route provides scenic views of the Beas River and snow-covered mountains.

Why Visit

Jogini Falls is perfect for trekking lovers, nature enthusiasts, and photographers. It offers a peaceful escape from crowded tourist spots and gives a refreshing experience in nature.

Nearby Places

Vashisht Temple, Beas River, Old Manali, Nehru Kund
