🌊 Nehru Kund

Location

Nehru Kund is located about 5 kilometers from Manali on the Manali-Leh Highway. It lies on the way to Rohtang Pass and is surrounded by mountains and valleys.

History & Importance

The place is named after Jawaharlal Nehru, who used to drink water from this natural spring. The water is believed to originate from Bhrigu Lake and is known for its purity.

Features

Nehru Kund is famous for its clear and cold natural spring water. The surrounding landscape includes snow-covered mountains, green valleys, and flowing streams. It is a calm and scenic spot.

Why Visit

It is a peaceful destination for relaxation and photography. Visitors can enjoy fresh air, natural beauty, and a refreshing break during their journey.

Nearby Places

Rohtang Pass, Solang Valley, Jogini Falls, Bhrigu Lake
