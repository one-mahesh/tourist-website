🏛 Museum of Himachal Culture & Folk Art

Location

The Museum of Himachal Culture & Folk Art is located near Hadimba Temple in Manali. It is easily accessible and lies in a culturally rich area.

History & Importance

The museum was established to preserve and showcase the traditional culture, lifestyle, and heritage of Himachal Pradesh. It provides insight into the history and customs of the region.

Features

The museum displays a wide collection of traditional costumes, musical instruments, household items, weapons, and artifacts. It also shows models of old houses and temples, giving a glimpse of ancient life.

Why Visit

It is an ideal place for learning about Himachali culture and traditions. Visitors can understand the lifestyle of local people and explore historical artifacts.

Nearby Places

Hadimba Temple, Mall Road, Old Manali, Van Vihar
