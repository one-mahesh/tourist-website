📍 Location

Ayodhya is located in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh on the banks of the Sarayu River. The city is easily accessible by rail and road from major Indian cities.

📚 History & Importance

Ayodhya is one of the oldest cities in India and holds great religious importance in Hinduism. It is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama, a major deity in the epic Ramayana. The city has been a center of pilgrimage for centuries, attracting millions of devotees annually.

✨ Features

Ayodhya is known for its temples, ghats, and spiritual atmosphere with sacred sites on the Sarayu River.

  • Ram Mandir - Grand temple dedicated to Lord Rama with modern architecture
  • Hanuman Garhi - Ancient temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman
  • Sarayu River Ghats - Sacred bathing and evening aarti ceremonies
  • Kanak Bhawan - Historic temple with beautiful courtyard

🎯 Why Visit Ayodhya?

Ayodhya is ideal for spiritual tourism and religious pilgrimage. Visitors experience devotion, rituals, and a calm religious environment.

  • Spiritual Journey - Birthplace of Lord Rama with profound religious significance
  • Temple Exploration - Historic temples with devotional atmosphere
  • River Rituals - Sacred ceremonies and evening aarti at Sarayu Ghats
  • Pilgrimage Experience - One of Hinduism's holiest sites
