🏞 Old Manali

Location

Old Manali is located about 3 kilometers uphill from Manali town, separated by the Manalsu River.

History & Importance

Old Manali is known for its traditional village lifestyle and cultural heritage. It represents the old charm of Himachal Pradesh.

Features

The area is famous for apple orchards, wooden houses, cafes, and peaceful surroundings. It has a relaxed atmosphere compared to the busy New Manali.

Why Visit

Old Manali is perfect for those who want peace, scenic beauty, and a laid-back environment. It is popular among backpackers and nature lovers.

Nearby Places

Hadimba Temple, Jogini Falls, Mall Road, Vashisht Village
