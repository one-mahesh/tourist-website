🛕 Hadimba Devi Temple

Location

Hadimba Devi Temple is located in a dense cedar forest in Manali, Himachal Pradesh. It is about 2 kilometers from Mall Road.

History & Importance

The temple is dedicated to Hidimba Devi, the wife of Bhima from the Mahabharata. Built in 1553, it is an important religious site and attracts devotees from all over India.

Features

The temple has a unique wooden architecture with a pagoda-style roof. It is surrounded by tall cedar trees, creating a peaceful atmosphere. The carvings and structure reflect traditional Himachali design.

Why Visit

Visitors come here for spiritual peace, architectural beauty, and natural surroundings. It is one of the most famous tourist attractions in Manali.

Nearby Places

Mall Road, Old Manali, Van Vihar, Museum of Himachal Culture
